Psychological Test Administration

Consultation

For GENERAL concerns, inquiry or follow-up. Counselors help students create solutions to recognized concern/issue by giving adept advice and accessible resources that can help improve the situation.

Counseling

PROCESS

How it works

Getting started with GCO Connect is simple and straightforward

01 · Service Type

Select the type of service you need from the Guidance and Counseling Office.

02 · Basic Info

Provide your basic details so the counselor can understand your situation and contact you.

03 · Schedule Booking

Choose a convenient date and time for your session with the Guidance and Counseling Office.

04 · Appointment Details

Review the details of your booking, including the service, counselor, and schedule.

05 · Completed

Attend your session and continue working with your counselor as needed.
